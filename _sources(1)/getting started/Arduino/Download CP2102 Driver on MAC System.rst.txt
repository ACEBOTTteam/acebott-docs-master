Download CP2102 Driver on MAC System
======================================

**Download CP2102 driver**

`Download CP2102 driver <https://www.dropbox.com/scl/fo/e1ogaizxh5s4xm9fbiywf/AH-oDDo70sKkYdkpYK086bM?rlkey=cbpff8tenoxmxgz5gem3d2r12&st=6m621k5p&dl=0>`_


**Installation**

Open "Mac System" folder, then open "Mac_OSX_VCP_Driver" folder, and then double-click "SiLabsUSBDriverDisk.dmg" installation file, according to the following installation window prompts to install.

.. figure:: /../images/Mac_CP2102_driver-1.png
    :align: center

Click "Continue".

.. figure:: /../images/Mac_CP2102_driver-2.png
    :align: center

Click "Agree".

.. figure:: /../images/Mac_CP2102_driver-3.png
    :align: center

Your Mac may be prompted for a password for installation, so enter your login password.

.. figure:: /../images/Mac_CP2102_driver-4.png
    :align: center

This prompt indicates that the driver has been installed.

.. figure:: /../images/Mac_CP2102_driver-5.png
    :align: center

Then enter Arduino IDE, you can see that the software recognizes the serial port.

.. figure:: /../images/Mac_CP2102_driver-6.png
    :align: center
