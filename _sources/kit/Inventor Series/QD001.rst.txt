QD001 Quadruped Bionic Spider Robot for ESP8266

一、Product Introduction
------

Quadruped Bionic Spider Robot is a creative learning kit designed for beginners.

With this suite, you can have a good understanding of the walking posture of the spider, and through the programming design of robot action, master the control of multi-degree of freedom robot.
